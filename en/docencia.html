INTRODUCTORIO

Jordi Ros i Jordi Sutrias van obtenir el títol d’arquitectes per l’Escola Tècnica Superior d’Arquitectura de Barcelona (ETSAB). Ambdós compaginen la seva activitat professional amb la docència universitària.

5.1.1

Degree of architect, by the Escola Tècnica Superior d’Arquitectura de Barcelona (ETSAB) 1983.

Associated Professor at the Department of Architectural Design (ETSAB), 1983-1990.

Professor at the International Laboratory Architecture and Urban Design (Siena,1985).

Full professor at the Department of Architectural Design (ETSAB), since 1990.

Vice-Dean of the ETSAB, 1989-1992.

Vice-Dean of the Centre for Design Studies of the UPC, 1989-1990.

President of a Diploma’s Work Jury at the ETSAB, since1991.

Editor of the review DPA of the UPC, since1996.

Head of the Department of Architectural Design at the ETSAB, 1996-1998.

Conferences about his own work at different European schools of architecture (London, Nàpoli, Sarajevo, Milano, Venezia, Mendrisio, Prague, Budapest and Wien).

Head of Atelier Studio “Projects IX and X” at ETSAB (fifth year), since 1999.

Visiting research at IIT of Chicago. Illinois, 2007.

Vice-Dean of the ETSAB, since 2005.
